1. Don't get the L-CAR9

2. Don't play on hard maps

3. You can only carry four perks (unless you have are some cheating no life who has a bunch of ridiculously over powered, strong, game breaking gobble gums), so choose your perks wisely. I recommend Juggernog, Widow's Wine, Speed-Cola, Mule Kick, and Stamin-Up.

4. Try to get and keep Juggernog before round 10

5. Manage your points well...

Woah! What a cool transition into my next topic, points!

6. On Black Ops 2 and 3, you can prone in front of a perk machine you can get extra points. Doesn't matter if power is on or off

7. I wouldn't activate traps until after round 10

8. Knifing zombies on round one is an instant kill, and killing zombies with a knife gives you more points, so don't use your gun at all on round 1

9. On round two, shoot a zombie 4 or 5 times then knife it, it will give you more points and you will conserve ammo.

10. THE RAYGUN IS NOT AS GOOD AS IT WAS IN EARLY COD ZOMBIES!!! THE RAYGUN ISN'T SPECIAL ANYMORE!!!

11. To all of you 9 year olds, if a gun is automatic, it doesn't mean its op

12. Training isn't very reliable on Cold War zombies. The zombies have different behavior, which sometimes leads to zombies not chasing after you.

13. If you camp to much, sometimes it will lead to you not being able to get back out of your spot, so only camp for about 3 rounds, drink perks, Pack a Pucnch, etc. Then find a new spot to camp in.

14. The first thing you should try to achieve as soon as you load into a map is to try and get the power on as perks and a few doors won't be available.

15. Pack a Punch after the round is over, because then you won't have zombies swarming around you.

16. Don't buy Cold War on apc with an AMD Radeon with Vega 8 graphics card, I speak with experience.

17. Training is very reliable (unless your playing cold war), practice your training on the Ascension podieum

18. Think about what gun you want to Pack a Punch because after you upgrade a trash gun, it will become an average gun.
